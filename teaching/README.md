# Teaching · Pre-Environs

Open, reproducible teaching materials in **spatial epidemiology**, aligned with the project's research lines on environments and health.

| Seminar | Level | Stack | Source |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Disease mapping — childhood obesity in Palma** | 2nd-year Nursing | Quarto · R (`sf`, `leaflet`) | [`disease-mapping/`](disease-mapping/) |

Health data are **synthetic** (calibrated to real Balearic prevalences); area-level deprivation/income covariates and geometry are **real** (SEE IP2011; INE ADRH; INE census sections). See each seminar's `data/README.md` for details.
